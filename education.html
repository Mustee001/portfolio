Educational Background

I am currently a 400-level undergraduate at Ibrahim Badamasi Babangida University, Lapai (IBBUL), Niger State, where I study Computer Science. My academic journey has significantly influenced my passion for technology, driving me to explore key areas such as programming, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity.

In addition to my university education, I am actively enrolled in the Power Learn Project (PLP) — a continent-wide digital empowerment initiative focused on software development and tech career advancement. Through this program, I continue to strengthen my skills via structured learning and hands-on projects.

While I am still growing in the field, I consistently apply my knowledge through practical, real-world challenges that deepen my experience and technical ability.
