Voluntary & Community Engagement

During my Industrial Training at Kainji Lake National Park, where I was part of the Planning, Research, and ICT Department, I also volunteered with the Green Bond Project — an environmental initiative focused on tree planting and ecosystem restoration in local communities.

Although the project wasn't directly tech-related, I took pride in contributing to sustainable development through active participation with the planting team. This experience highlights my dedication to community growth, my willingness to learn beyond my core field, and my drive to be part of purposeful, impact-driven work.
